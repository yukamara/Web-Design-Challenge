City Latitude vs. Maximum Temperature (01/05/17)

As expected, the weather becomes more significantly warmar as one approaches the equator (0 degree Latitude). More interestingly, however, is the fact that the southern hemisphere tends to be warmer this time of year than the Northern hemisphere. This my be due to the tilt of the earth at the time of the year this data was gethered.
